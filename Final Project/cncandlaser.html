What is this page about

As the name suggest. This page is for the parts I C&Ced and lasercut for my final project at EP1001

Robot arm holder Model

Using a ruler and caliper, I measured my arm and made the model out in fusion

Files to download

Please ensure to use a 20mm board for the cnc part as the pocket dept is 15mm. The Laser cut parts are 3mm thick
Click here to download the C&C DXF file.
Click here to download the laser cut DXF file

Post Processing

Using the orbit sander, sand the surface of the holder and remove all sharp edges on the holder

Things to take note!

As printing this may case the printing time to increase, It is much advisable to laser cut this out before super glueing this part to the arm.
This would allow future modifications to be made. For my presentation, I would only be proving that 2 fingers work for the sake of time.
